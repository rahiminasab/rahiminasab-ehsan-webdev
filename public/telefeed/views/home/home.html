Feedback for Telegram Channels

It's all about Feedback

Telegram channels are single voiced tools which broadcast their owner's word to channel members. While members can benefit from reading these posts, they cannot easily deliver their feedback to channel owners. TeleFeed provide this opportunity for both channel owner and members, to benefit from each others feedback and opinions.
